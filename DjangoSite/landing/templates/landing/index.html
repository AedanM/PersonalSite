Programming Experience

CodeStats is an free tracking service for programmers which logs experience programming in different languages. I started my logging in October of 2023. It works by associating each keypress in code editors with the language of the file. While not a perfect representation of ability, it does show experience and usage trends for my developing, both professional and personal.

Using a library from @byjokese my experience gained in the last 12 months is shown as a monthly breakdown with my historic top 6 languages by experience. The status bars represent the progress towards the next "level" in the language.
